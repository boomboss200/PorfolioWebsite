    About Watkins2.0

    Teacher's Assistant

    Proceded Development as Teacher's Assistant

    Native Development

    Natively for iPads and iPhones
    For Devices Above IOS 15.5

    The iOS app, designed with Swift and SwiftUI, serves as a step counter that calculates the distance covered based on steps taken per unit time. Leveraging the Core Motion framework, the app utilizes the device's motion sensors to provide real-time monitoring of the user's physical activity. Tailored for self-assessment, especially in the context of normal pressure hydrocephalus (NPH), the app enables users to gain insights into their activity patterns. By offering accurate step counting and distance calculation, it promotes a proactive approach to health management. The app exemplifies the synergy of cutting-edge iOS development and its impactful application in the healthcare domain.
